What is Media, Information, and Technoculture?

Created in 1997, the MIT (Media, Information, and Technoculture) program at Western University educates students about the world around them. Media and messages surround us and being able to engage with and understand the messages we receive is critical in today's day and age. Students in the program get exposure to marketing, social justice, economics, and journalism.

What can I do with a degree in Media, Information and Technoculture?

MIT students have gone off to work in industries like: Journalism, Marketing, Advertising, Public Relations, Communications, and even Not-for-Profits.

D-List Celebrity Alumni

Some "famous" MIT alumni include: Liz Trineer, a former MUCH MUSIC VJ, works as an entertainment reporter for etalk. Liz has interviewed some of the biggest names in entertainment like Justin Bieber and Ed Sheeran and learned what it took to be an on-air personality while studying MIT at Western.
